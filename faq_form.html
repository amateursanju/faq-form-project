DAITM offers the following full-time courses: BBA, BCA, BBA in Hospital Management, B.Sc in Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT). For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

Yes, the BBA, BCA and BBA in Hospital Management are courses approved by AICTE. Also, BBA, BCA, BBA in Hospital Management and B.Sc in Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT) is affiliated to Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

The BBA program provides a foundation in key business disciplines — such as marketing, finance and HR — along with practical exposure via projects, internships, workshops, and industry interactions. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

The BCA program helps in building a foundation in computer science & IT – Programming Languages, Problem Solving and Analytical skills along with practical exposure to labs, projects, workshops via internships and industry interactions. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

The BBA in Hospital Management helps in understanding how hospitals and healthcare organizations operate - hospital administration, patient-care coordination, and healthcare services work. Helps develop skills in administrative and managerial roles in healthcare industries through exposures in projects, internships and industry interactions. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

The B.Sc MLT program offers education in clinical laboratory techniques, diagnostic procedures, and the use of advanced medical equipment — preparing students for roles in medical labs and patient-care diagnostics. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

All these honours courses are for a duration of 4 years and each year is divided into 2 semesters. A Student will have to clear all the 8 semesters to complete the education in DAITM. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

Yes. For example, the BBA, BCA, BBA in Hospital Management and B.Sc in Medical Lab technology offers a solid base to all those who would like to take up higher studies like MBA, MCA, MHA, M.Sc in Medical Lab technology. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

BBA, BCA each has 120 intakes and BBA in Hospital Management has 60 intakes. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

There are 30 intakes for the B.Sc in Medical Laboratory Technology program. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

Admissions take place annually as per affiliating University specified guidelines. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

Interested candidates should fill the application form available on DAITM’s official website and complete the required payment to secure admission. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

Admissions are generally merit-based (based on qualifying exam organized by MAKAUT). For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.

No, DAITM does not provide postgraduate admissions. However, the course curriculum, classes, infrastructure and events prepare them in a way so that pursuing Postgraduate later becomes easy. For further details please visit www.daitm.org.in.
