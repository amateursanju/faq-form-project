DAITM Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What B.Tech specializations are offered?

A: We offer specializations in Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, and Electronics & Communication Engineering.

Q: Do you offer any integrated BBA/MBA programs?

A: Yes, we have a 5-year integrated program covering both BBA and MBA degrees.

Q: Are the courses AICTE approved?

A: All our engineering and management programs are fully approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Q: How frequently is the curriculum updated?

A: The curriculum is reviewed and updated every two years in consultation with industry experts.

Q: Is there flexibility to switch courses in the first year?

A: Course switching is permitted only during the first two weeks of the semester, subject to availability and merit.

Q: What is the minimum eligibility for B.Tech admission?

A: A minimum of 60% marks in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) is required.

Q: Is an entrance exam mandatory for all courses?

A: Yes, admission to B.Tech requires a valid score in the state-level entrance exam or JEE Mains.

Q: When does the application process usually start?

A: The application window typically opens in May and closes by the end of June.

Q: What documents are required for counseling?

A: You need 10th and 12th mark sheets, entrance exam scorecard, domicile certificate, and five passport-sized photos.

Q: Do you offer direct admission based on merit?

A: A limited number of management seats are available for direct admission based on 10+2 merit, without an entrance exam.

Q: What is the average placement package offered?

A: The average placement package for the last academic year was INR 4.5 Lakhs per annum (LPA).

Q: What is the highest salary package achieved?

A: The highest package recorded in the last batch was INR 18 LPA, secured by a Computer Science student.

Q: Which top companies recruit from DAITM?

A: Major recruiters include TCS, Infosys, Wipro, L&T, and various tech startups.

Q: Does the college offer placement training?

A: Yes, we have dedicated soft skills training, technical aptitude coaching, and mock interview sessions starting from the 3rd semester.

Q: What is the overall placement percentage?

A: We consistently maintain a placement rate of over 85% for eligible students.

Q: Are the classrooms air-conditioned?

A: All seminar halls and final-year classrooms are air-conditioned, with standard ventilation in others.

Q: Is Wi-Fi available throughout the campus?

A: Yes, 24/7 high-speed Wi-Fi is available in all academic blocks, hostels, and common areas.

Q: How extensive is the central library?

A: The library spans two floors and contains over 50,000 volumes, national/international journals, and a digital subscription library.

Q: Are there dedicated labs for R&D?

A: Yes, the college features specialized labs for robotics, cloud computing, and advanced manufacturing research.

Q: What sports facilities are available?

A: We have a large cricket and football ground, a basketball court, and indoor facilities for table tennis and badminton.

Q: Are hostels compulsory for out-of-town students?

A: Hostels are optional, but highly recommended for all out-of-town students.

Q: How many cultural fests are held annually?

A: We host two major annual fests: 'TechNova' (technical) and 'Kalakriti' (cultural), along with several smaller departmental events.

Q: What is the student-teacher ratio?

A: We maintain a healthy student-teacher ratio of 15:1 to ensure personalized attention.

Q: Are there different student clubs?

A: Yes, we have over 15 active clubs, including robotics, coding, photography, and literature clubs.

Q: What medical facilities are on campus?

A: We have a dedicated on-campus health center with a full-time nurse and an on-call doctor available 24 hours.

Q: What is the total tuition fee for B.Tech?

A: The current annual tuition fee is INR 1,20,000, excluding hostel and mess charges.

Q: Are there any scholarship schemes available?

A: Yes, merit-based scholarships are available for students with a ranking above 5000 in the entrance exam.

Q: Is there an installment payment option for fees?

A: Fees can be paid in two installments per academic year, one at the start of each semester.

Q: What is the one-time enrollment fee?

A: There is a non-refundable one-time enrollment and security deposit fee of INR 15,000.

Q: How do I access my official fee receipt?

A: All official fee receipts are issued digitally and can be downloaded from the student portal within 48 hours of payment.
